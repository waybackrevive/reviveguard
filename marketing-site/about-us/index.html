Our Story

We built this because
we lived the problem.

We run WaybackRevive — a WordPress recovery service that has rescued hundreds of broken, hacked, and neglected sites. After years of fixing the same preventable disasters, we built ReviveGuard: proactive managed protection so your site never needs recovering in the first place.

Why We Built This

WaybackRevive fixes sites.
ReviveGuard keeps them safe.

WaybackRevive is our WordPress recovery service — it's still running and still helping site owners recover from crashes, hacks, plugin conflicts, and failed updates. Over years of that work, a pattern emerged: almost every site we rescued had the same preventable problems. No backups. Outdated plugins. Unmonitored uptime. An SSL certificate nobody noticed expiring.

So we built ReviveGuard as a separate product — proactive, ongoing managed care that prevents those disasters before they happen. WaybackRevive is the emergency room. ReviveGuard is the reason you never end up there.

We capped ReviveGuard at 26 managed sites deliberately. Not because we can't scale — because quality managed care means actually knowing your sites. Every client relationship is personal. Every monthly report is written by a human who knows your setup.

What We Stand For

Our values shape every decision.

From the client cap to the monthly reports, every choice we make comes back to these principles.

🔒 Quality over volume

We deliberately cap managed sites at 26. More clients would mean less attention per site. We'd rather be exceptional for a small number of people than mediocre for hundreds.

🔍 Transparency first

Every action we take on your site is logged and visible in your portal. Every monthly report is written honestly — we tell you what changed, what was flagged, and what we did about it. No mystery, no vague "maintenance done" emails.

🧠 Prevention, not reaction

Our entire service philosophy is built on preventing problems before they happen. Monitoring every 5 minutes, proactive backups, SSL alerts, and plugin updates — not waiting for something to break before we notice.

🤝 Real relationships

You're not a ticket number. We know your sites, your business, and your history with us. Support goes to one place — hello@reviveguard.com — and a real person responds.

📋 Honest pricing

Flat monthly pricing, no surprises. What you see on the pricing page is exactly what you pay. No setup fees hidden in fine print, no "base price + mandatory add-ons" tricks.

⚙️ Zero unnecessary complexity

Our plugin installs in 60 seconds. The portal is designed to be understood in minutes. Managed WordPress care shouldn't require you to become a WordPress expert — that's our job.

The 26 Client Cap

Why we limit how many
sites we manage.

Most WordPress care services sign up hundreds or thousands of clients, then automate everything and call it "managed." You get a shared dashboard, automated emails, and nobody who actually knows your site.

We made a different choice. By capping our managed sites at 26, we ensure every client gets genuine attention. When something unusual happens on your site, we notice — because we know what "normal" looks like for your specific setup.

When a spot opens, WaybackRevive alumni are first in line. After that, new clients go through a real evaluation — not an automated sign-up flow. We only accept sites we're confident we can protect well.
