The Reality

Your website is one missed renewal away from disappearing

We've seen it hundreds of times at WaybackRevive — the site goes dark, the phone stops ringing, years of content vanish overnight. Almost every time, it was completely preventable.

Risk 01

Domain expires quietly

No warning. Your domain expires, a squatter grabs it, and your business address disappears overnight. We alert you at 60, 30, and 7 days before expiry — automatically.

Risk 02

Outdated WordPress = open door

90% of hacked WordPress sites were running outdated plugins. Every day without updates is a day attackers can walk straight in. We update everything, automatically, on your plan's schedule.

Risk 03

Hosting backups aren't enough

Your host "backs up daily" — but their backups live on the same server. Restoring costs $150+ and takes days. We store yours in independent cloud storage and restore in under 4 hours on Shield.
