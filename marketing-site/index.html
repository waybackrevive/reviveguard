After your account is set up, we send you a lightweight WordPress plugin and a unique API token generated specifically for your site. You install the plugin (takes under 60 seconds), paste your token in the plugin's settings page, and it connects immediately. No server access required, no SSH, no technical knowledge. The plugin checks in with our platform every 5 minutes and picks up commands from our system.

Yes — your portal supports multiple sites. Use the "+ Add Website" wizard in your portal to register additional sites. If you're an agency or freelancer managing multiple client sites, this is designed exactly for you.

Before every update run, we take a full site backup. If something breaks after the update, we restore from that pre-update snapshot. You'll see both events logged in your portal — the update and the restore. Guard and Shield plans include this protection automatically. The restore process typically takes under 30 minutes for Guard clients and is guaranteed within 4 hours for Shield clients.

All backups are stored in Backblaze B2 — a geographically redundant cloud storage service completely independent from your hosting provider. Even if your host goes offline, gets sold, or has a catastrophic failure, your backups are in a separate location we control. This is a critical distinction: most hosting "backup" plans store the backup on the same server as your site.

Hosting backups are stored on the same infrastructure as your site — if the host has a problem, you may lose both the site and the backup simultaneously. ReviveGuard is a complete managed service, not just cloud storage. The backup is one piece of a system that runs actively to prevent problems rather than just recover from them.
